Atma Jyoti Yoga Retreat & Sound Healing Center

Join our internationally accredited 200-hour and 300-hour Yoga Teacher Training Courses (YTTC) at Atma Jyoti Yoga Retreat and Sound Healing Center in the heart of Pokhara, Nepal—a serene sanctuary near the Himalayas. These immersive trainings are Yoga Alliance certified, offering students a solid foundation or advanced mastery in both practical yoga and theoretical knowledge.

Led by a team of highly experienced yoga teachers from India and Nepal, our programs go far beyond asana practice. Each course is a holistic journey into the depth of yoga philosophy, functional anatomy, professional teaching skills, and conscious living.

Why Choose Atma Jyoti Yoga Teacher Training?

  • ✅ Yoga Alliance USA Certification – Earn your internationally recognized certificate upon completion of either the 200-hour or 300-hour training.
  • ✅ Expert Teachers from India, Nepal & Europe – Traditional knowledge meets modern insight through our diverse and seasoned faculty.
  • ✅ Balanced Curriculum – In-depth training in asana, pranayama, meditation, alignment, sequencing, and adjustments, supported by detailed modules in yoga anatomy, yoga philosophy, teaching methodology and professional ethics.
  • ✅ Professional Development – Learn how to teach, build your career, and ethically share yoga worldwide.
  • ✅ Ayurveda Nutrition & Holistic Wellness – Discover the sister science of yoga with guidance on Ayurvedic health, nutrition, and lifestyle to support a yogic life.
  • ✅ Sound Healing Integration – Unique to our school, experience the healing power of Himalayan sound bowls and vibrational therapy as part of your inner and outer transformation.

Our Courses

  • ✦ 200-Hour Yoga Teacher Training — Perfect for beginners and aspiring teachers, this foundational course will establish your personal practice and prepare you to teach yoga safely and effectively anywhere in the world.
  • ✦ 300-Hour Advanced Yoga Teacher Training — Designed for those who have completed a 200-hour training, our 300-hour course offers deeper insights into yoga philosophy, therapeutic application, subtle body studies, and advanced sequencing, refining your skills as a confident and knowledgeable teacher.
