Why Choose Atma Jyoti for Sound Bowl Healing Training?

  • ✅ Authentic Lineage-Based Curriculum — Grounded in traditional Himalayan and shamanic vibrational principles, our teaching connects ancient wisdom with practical, therapeutic application.
  • ✅ Expert Guidance — Learn directly from Mahendra Mahat, whose depth of knowledge springs from a lifetime immersed in healing arts, supported by a skilled team of practitioners.
  • ✅ Hands-On, Practical Learning — Move beyond theory through extensive practice with genuine, hand-hammered singing bowls. Develop confidence in playing techniques and session facilitation.
  • ✅ Professional Certification — Graduate with a recognized certificate from Atma Jyoti Yoga Retreat & Sound Healing Center, equipping you to integrate sound healing into your wellness practice or to offer standalone sound therapy sessions.

  • ✅ Immersion in Nature — Study in Pokhara, where the tranquil environment and panoramic mountain views provide an ideal setting for deep listening and resonant practice.
