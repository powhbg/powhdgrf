Volunteer with Us

Atma Jyoti welcomes volunteers who are drawn to simple, intentional living rooted in wellness, devotion, and genuine community. If you're looking to contribute meaningfully while immersing yourself in the beauty of Lakeside Pokhara, we'd love to have you with us.

Who We're Looking For

We ask for a minimum stay of 30 days and a shared commitment to the values that shape life here ‚Äî simplicity, kindness, and a spirit of service.

General Volunteer Program

Volunteers contribute around 4 hours of hands-on support each day. We particularly welcome those with skills in digital marketing, social media, and online promotion ‚Äî helping Atma Jyoti grow its presence and reach is a meaningful and valued contribution. Additional areas of support include gardening and outdoor work, cooking and meal preparation, cleaning and daily upkeep, and light maintenance.

Yoga Teacher Volunteer Program

Experienced yoga teachers are warmly invited to lead sessions and workshops, promote classes locally and online, and help grow awareness of Atma Jyoti's offerings within the community. English fluency is required. In recognition of your contribution, yoga teachers can choose the arrangement that works best for them ‚Äî either a 50% commission on every class taught, or free accommodation and meals in exchange for teaching.

What We Offer

All volunteers receive a private twin or double room with shared bathroom, and three daily meals prepared and shared together using fresh, organic ingredients. More than anything, you become part of a spiritual community rooted in human connection ‚Äî and that, we find, is what people carry with them long after they leave.

A Few Things We Ask

We simply ask that you respect the daily rhythm and community guidelines, treat everyone ‚Äî guests, residents, and fellow volunteers ‚Äî with kindness and compassion, contribute with an open heart, and communicate any concerns with honesty and care.
