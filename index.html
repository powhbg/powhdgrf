Why choose ATMA JYOTI YOGA RETREAT & SOUND HEALING CENTER ?

Authentic Ashram-Style Environment

We operate on the traditional principles of an ashram. Learners become part of a teacher-student family, with a disciplined daily schedule, yogic lifestyle, regular practice, simplicity, and service.

Beautiful Natural Setting

Nestled in nature in the Annapurna region near Pokhara, with views of mountains, forest, hills, and Fewa Lake. Peaceful, quiet location away from city hustle, which aids meditation, reflection, and rest.

Comprehensive Holistic Program

We offer more than just yoga postures: meditation, pranayama (breathing practices), mantra & kirtan chanting, purification (Shatkarmas), and sound healing. Attention to emotional, spiritual, and energetic dimensions.

Food, Accommodation & Comfort

Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available. Meals made with fresh or organic local ingredients. Multiple accommodation choices: private, twin sharing, and private cottage with great views.

Guidance from Experienced Teachers

Our instructors are well-versed in various yogic traditions. Teachers have practiced from early age, experienced in Hatha, Ashtanga, meditation, and various spiritual disciplines.

Immersive, Transformative Experience

Our structure is designed for self-inquiry, mental clarity, emotional balance, and spiritual growth. Silent walks, self-study time, and sound healing sessions create a truly transformative journey.
